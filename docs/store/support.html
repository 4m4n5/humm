FAQ

How do I link with my partner?

One of you creates an account and finds the invite code in the app. The other signs up (or signs in) and enters that code on the link screen. After linking, you share the same couple space.

I forgot my password.

Use the password reset flow from the sign-in screen (Firebase Auth). If email does not arrive, check spam and that you typed the correct email.

Can I delete my data?

Yes. Email the address above and ask for account deletion. See the privacy policy for what we remove and what may linger in backups briefly.

Is there a public feed or social graph?

No. The app is for two linked people only; there is no discovery or public timeline.

Where is my data stored?

On Google Firebase (Authentication + Firestore). See the privacy policy for details.
