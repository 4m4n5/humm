Private rituals for two · support, privacy & product overview

Hum - rituals is a calm, invite-only space for you and your partner—not a social network. Link with a short code, then share small rituals: weighted quick spins, live battles, bi-annual awards (nominate → align → cheer), and reasons you love each other. Your data lives on Google Firebase; see the privacy policy for details.

What you can do

Home
Jump into quick spin, battle, awards, reasons, or your profile—everything is built for two people.
Decide — quick spin
Pick a category (food, activity, movie, …), set weights, spin, veto if you want a redo, and save picks to history.
Decide — battle
Build a shared pool, run a realtime bracket together, revote on ties, and let a coin break deadlocks.
Awards
Nominate stories by category, walk through alignment when you’re ready, resolve overlaps, cheer winners, and browse past seasons.
Reasons
Write a line for your partner and, when the moment’s right, draw three random reasons they’ve written about you.
You
XP, levels, badges, and a soft snapshot of how you’re showing up together.
