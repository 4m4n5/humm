Hum - rituals is a calm, invite-only space for you and your partner—not a social network. Link with a short code, then share small rituals: mood check-ins, shared habits, Pick Together (vote round by round, or pick for us when only one of you is around), bi-annual awards (nominate → align → cheer), and reasons you love each other. Your data lives on Google Firebase; see the privacy policy for details.

What you can do

Home
A time-aware greeting, your partner’s live presence, and quick access to everything. The ambient glow warms as your streaks grow together.
Mood
Check in with how you’re feeling throughout the day. See your partner’s mood in real time, track a seven-day strip, and celebrate “in sync” moments when you both feel the same way.
Habits
Create daily or weekly habits—shared or personal. Track completion side-by-side, watch a six-week heatmap track your shared progress, and build joint streaks. When both of you finish all shared habits, enjoy a celebration moment.
Decide — Pick Together
Auto-seeded shared pool per category. Vote together round by round when you’re both around, or tap pick for us for a one-tap pick when only one of you is. Calm, no randomness mechanics—just landing on a choice.
Awards
Nominate stories by category for each season. When you’re ready, move through align (private picks), resolve overlaps, cheer the winners, and keep past seasons in the vault.
Reasons
Write a line for your partner and, when the moment’s right, draw three random reasons they’ve written about you.
You
XP, levels, badges, weekly challenges, and a soft snapshot of how you’re showing up together.
