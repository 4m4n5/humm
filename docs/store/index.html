Hum - rituals is a calm, invite-only space for you and your partner—not a social network. Link with a short code, then share small rituals: mood check-ins, shared habits, weighted quick spins, live battles, bi-annual awards (nominate → align → cheer), and reasons you love each other. Your data lives on Google Firebase; see the privacy policy for details.

What you can do

Home
Jump into what matters today—everything is built for two people sharing small daily rituals.
Mood
Check in with how you’re feeling throughout the day. See your partner’s mood in real time, track a seven-day strip, and celebrate “in sync” moments when you both feel the same way.
Habits
Create daily or weekly habits—shared (together) or personal (just you). Track completion with participant pills, watch a six-week heatmap fill up, and build joint streaks. When both of you finish all shared habits, enjoy a celebration moment.
Decide — quick spin
Pick a category (food, activity, movie, …), set weights, spin, veto if you want a redo, and save picks to history.
Decide — battle
Build a shared pool, run a realtime bracket together, revote on ties, and let a coin break deadlocks.
Awards
Nominate stories by category for each season. When you’re ready, move through align (private picks), resolve overlaps, cheer the winners, and keep past seasons in the vault.
Reasons
Write a line for your partner and, when the moment’s right, draw three random reasons they’ve written about you.
You
XP, levels, badges, weekly challenges, and a soft snapshot of how you’re showing up together.
