Hum - rituals is your relationship’s private corner—not a social network. It is a calm, intimate space designed exclusively for two people to deepen their connection through shared daily habits. Link with a short invite code, then share small rituals: mood check-ins, shared habits, decide (vote together round by round, or pick for us from your curated list), bi-annual awards (nominate → align → cheer), and reasons you love each other. There are no mechanics of chance, no wagers, no public feed, and no ads. Your data lives on Google Firebase; see the privacy policy for details.

What you can do

Home
A time-aware greeting, your partner’s live presence, and quick access to everything. The ambient glow warms as your streaks grow together.
Mood
Check in with how you’re feeling throughout the day. See your partner’s mood in real time, track a seven-day strip, and celebrate “in sync” moments when you both feel the same way.
Habits
Create daily or weekly habits—shared or personal. Track completion side-by-side, watch a six-week heatmap track your shared progress, and build joint streaks. When both of you finish all shared habits, enjoy a celebration moment.
Decide
Build a shared pool of ideas per category (e.g. restaurants, weekend plans). Vote together round by round to find the perfect compromise, or tap pick for us to let the app suggest one from your curated list when you can’t decide. No mechanics of chance, no wagers—just landing on a choice.
Awards
Celebrate your favorite moments. Nominate little stories and inside jokes by category for each season. When you’re ready, align your private picks, cheer the winners together, and keep past seasons in your vault.
Reasons
A space for gratitude. Write a simple reason why you love them; when the moment’s right, they can reveal three reasons you’ve written about them.
You
Track your journey together. Earn XP, level up, unlock badges, and view a soft snapshot of how you’re showing up for each other.
