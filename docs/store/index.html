six rituals

small things, every day

one private space, six gentle rituals to share with the person you chose.

mood

how you’re feeling, in real time

check in throughout the day. see your partner’s mood live, track a seven-day strip, and celebrate when you both feel the same.

habits

build the life you want, together

shared or personal habits. a six-week heatmap shows your shared progress, joint streaks build, and finishing the day together earns a gentle celebration.

decide

land on a choice without the back-and-forth

a shared pool of ideas per category. vote together round by round, or tap pick for us to suggest one from your own list. no chance, no wagers — just a calm way to decide.

awards

your favourite moments, twice a year

nominate little stories and inside jokes. when the season closes, align your private picks, cheer the winners, and keep past seasons in your private vault.

reasons

a quiet place for gratitude

write a simple reason why you love them. when the moment’s right, reveal three reasons they’ve written about you. always private, always between two.

you

a soft snapshot of your journey

track your XP and weekly challenges, unlock badges, and see a gentle reflection of how you’re showing up for each other.

how it works

three steps in

  1. 01

    create your account

    email and password. that’s it. no contacts, no socials, no profile to fill out.

  2. 02

    link your partner

    share a short invite code. once they’re in, you share the same private space.

  3. 03

    share your rituals

    start with a mood. add a habit you both want to keep. write one reason. let the rest unfold.
