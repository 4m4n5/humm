humm - D "resonance" variants

All variations of the sine wave concept. Exploring: dots vs no dots, glow vs flat, stroke weight, amplitude, and asymmetry.

Glow note: Variants with glow use a baked gaussian blur filter. This is non-compliant with iOS 26 Liquid Glass (which adds depth dynamically), but works great for marketing assets. For App Store submission, use the flat version; Icon Composer adds its own depth.
